The Stories That Matter for Your Business

Lawsuit Claims AI Ignored Warnings About Dangerous User

A stalking victim is suing OpenAI, alleging that ChatGPT enabled her abuser's behavior by ignoring multiple warnings. For small business owners, this case underscores the importance of using AI responsibly and ensuring that customer interactions are secure. Being aware of potential risks in automation can help protect your business and customers.

Anthropic has temporarily banned the creator of OpenClaw from using its AI tool, following a recent pricing change.
