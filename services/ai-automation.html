Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need a technical team to maintain the automations after you build them?

No. We build automations that non-technical business owners and managers can understand and monitor. We also provide documentation and training so your team knows what to do if something needs to change. For ongoing maintenance, most clients either handle it themselves or engage us on a monthly retainer.

What tools and platforms do you work with?

We work with Make (Integromat), Zapier, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Slack, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe, Calendly, Typeform, and dozens of other platforms. If your tools have an API or a native Zapier/Make integration, we can connect them. We'll assess your existing stack and recommend the best approach.

How long does it take to see results from AI and automation?

Most clients see meaningful time savings within the first 30 days. The first automations we build are typically the highest-impact, easiest-to-implement ones — so you start seeing ROI fast. More complex automations and AI integrations may take 60–90 days to fully deploy and optimize.

We're not a tech company — is AI automation right for us?

Yes — and in fact, non-tech businesses often have the most to gain from automation because they're doing the most manual work. If you have any repetitive tasks, forms that go into spreadsheets, emails you send repeatedly, or data you copy between systems — automation will save you time and money.

What's the difference between using a chatbot and just having a contact form?

A contact form is passive — the visitor fills it out and waits. A chatbot is active — it immediately engages, answers questions, qualifies the lead, and can book an appointment or provide pricing information on the spot. Studies consistently show chatbots convert 2–4x more visitors than static contact forms, especially outside business hours.
