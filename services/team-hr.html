Team &
HR Systems

We build the people infrastructure that turns a group of employees into a functioning team — with clear roles, real accountability, structured hiring, and performance management that actually works.

What We Do

People problems in small businesses are almost always systems problems. When a team member is not performing, the root cause is usually one of three things: they do not understand exactly what they are supposed to do and what good looks like, they do not have a clear way to be held accountable for their results, or they were hired and onboarded in a way that set them up to struggle from the start. The fix for each of these is not more management intervention — it is better structure.

Role clarity is the foundation. We work with business owners to define every role in the business with precision — not vague job descriptions, but specific outcome statements that tell each person what they are responsible for producing, how that will be measured, and how their role connects to the business's overall goals. When every person on your team knows exactly what success looks like in their role, management becomes easier and accountability becomes natural rather than confrontational.

Accountability systems give role clarity its teeth. We build lightweight but consistent accountability frameworks appropriate for small business teams — weekly check-ins with a standard format, scorecard reviews that track the specific metrics each role is responsible for, and a culture of addressing performance gaps early rather than letting them accumulate. These systems do not require HR software or formal performance management infrastructure. They require clear expectations, consistent measurement, and the discipline to have honest conversations when standards are not being met.

Hiring and onboarding processes are where many small businesses lose significant value without realizing it. A poor hire costs time, money, team morale, and often clients. Most small businesses hire reactively — when they need someone, they post a job, review applications quickly, and make a decision under pressure. We build structured hiring processes that include clearly defined role profiles, competency-based interview frameworks, practical assessment components, and reference check systems that actually produce useful information. When a hire is made, a structured onboarding process ensures they become productive quickly and understand the culture, expectations, and systems they are operating within.

Performance tracking closes the loop. We build the metrics and review cadences that allow managers to identify performance issues early, recognize and reinforce strong performance, and make promotion and compensation decisions based on objective evidence rather than gut feel or political dynamics. For small businesses, this does not need to be complex — it needs to be consistent.
