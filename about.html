Our Story

Built by Business Owners
Who Lived It First

Nexvora was not built in a boardroom. It was built by two entrepreneurs who ran a real business, felt the real pain, and decided to fix it for others.

Where We Come From
Started in Home Services. Built for Every Business.

Before Nexvora, our founders ran 24 25 CLEANERS — a home service business that grew to serve over 20,000 jobs and thousands of customers across the country. They know what it means to manage a real team, handle difficult customers, deal with cash flow, and try to grow without losing control.

They also know what it feels like to be the only person who knows how everything works — and what happens when you try to scale without systems. They lived through the chaos, made the mistakes, and eventually built the systems that saved them.

Along the way they implemented AI and automation tools inside their own company — not because it was trendy, but because they had no choice. It saved their time, reduced their costs, and gave them their lives back. That experience became the foundation of Nexvora.

They started Nexvora because they met too many small business owners going through the same stress they had been through — and realized most of them just needed someone who actually understood the problem, not a consultant who had only ever worked at a corporation.
