Sales Systems
38 · Fix First
The issue
You have no consistent follow-up process when leads do not convert immediately. When someone asks for a quote and goes quiet, they are likely gone forever.
Why it matters
Home services businesses lose 25–40% of potential revenue to unconverted leads. At your current revenue range, that is $75K–$120K per year sitting in a leaking bucket. Every lead you paid to generate that goes cold is money you spent twice — once to get them, once to replace them.
How to fix it — 3 paths
Save time: A 3-message automated follow-up sequence runs without you touching it. Set it up once, it works forever.
Save money: Recovering 20% of cold leads means spending less on new lead generation. Your cost per customer drops immediately.
Scale without you: Right now this depends on you remembering to follow up. A system does it whether you are in the office, on a job, or asleep.
