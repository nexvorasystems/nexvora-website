Q19What do your team members earn on average per month?Chips ×5
owner_hourly = owner_monthly_pay ÷ (weekly_hours × 4.33) team_hourly = team_monthly_pay ÷ (45 × 4.33) difference = ((team_hourly - owner_hourly) / owner_hourly) × 100%

Result shown as: "Your team earns X% more per hour than you — with zero risk, zero liability, and no weekends." Always ends with the "this is just the math" closing paragraph.

AI (1 call per assessment)

  • Benchmark narrative: 2–3 sentence motivational blockquote in Section 2
  • Executive summary: 3–4 sentences at top of Section 4 — what's strong, what's the main risk, what's the priority
  • Fallback: If AI fails, template text is shown. Report still fully works.

Email

  • Sent automatically when report page loads
  • Subject: "Your Nexvora Business Health Report — [Business Name]"
  • Footer: "Check your spam folder if you don't see this within 5 minutes."
  • API: Resend or SendGrid (confirmed during implementation)

PDF Download

  • Uses browser window.print() with print CSS
  • Hides nav, buttons, countdown timer in print view
  • Page breaks between sections · Footer with logo + date
