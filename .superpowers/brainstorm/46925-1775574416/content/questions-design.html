Design Section 1: The 15 Questions

Profile questions first (1–5), then diagnostic (6–15). Each question appears one at a time in chat style. Click any to expand and see the input type.

PROFILE — sets up benchmarks
Q1 — What industry is your business in?
Input: Chips — 8 industry options (Home Services, Construction, Food & Bev, Retail, Health & Wellness, Professional Services, Auto, Real Estate)
Q2 — What state is your business based in?
Input: Scrollable chip grid of all 50 states
Q3 — How long has your business been operating?
Input: Chips — Less than 1 year / 1–2 years / 3–5 years / 6–10 years / 10+ years
Q4 — What is your approximate annual revenue?
Input: Chips — Under $100K / $100K–$300K / $300K–$750K / $750K–$2M / Over $2M
Q5 — How many people work in your business (including you)?
Input: Chips — Just me / 2–5 / 6–15 / 16–30 / 30+
DIAGNOSTIC — scores 9 business areas
Q6 — Operations: Do you have documented processes for how your business runs?
Chips — Nothing documented / Key steps in my head / Some basic notes / Yes, written SOPs / Full documented + trained team
Q7 — Revenue: What percentage of your revenue comes from repeat customers?
Number input — type a % (0–100)
Q8 — Sales: What happens after someone shows interest but doesn't buy immediately?
Chips — Nothing, they're lost / I follow up manually sometimes / I have a set follow-up process / Automated follow-up system
Q9 — Marketing: How do most new customers find you?
Chips — Word of mouth / Google search / Social media / Paid ads / Other
Q10 — Customer Experience: What is your average online review rating?
Chips — No reviews yet / 1–3 stars / 3–4 stars / 4–4.5 stars / 4.5–5 stars
Q11 — AI & Automation: Which of these does your team still do manually every week?
Multi-select chips — Scheduling / Invoicing / Customer follow-up / Reporting / Data entry / None — mostly automated
Q12 — Team & HR: How do you currently manage team performance?
Chips — No formal process / Informal check-ins / Regular 1-on-1s / KPIs and tracking / Formal reviews + accountability system
Q13 — Financial: How often do you review your business financials?
Chips — Rarely / At tax time only / Quarterly / Monthly / Weekly
Q14 — Reporting: Do you track key business numbers in a dashboard or regular report?
Chips — No tracking / I check occasionally / Basic spreadsheet / Software dashboard / Real-time automated reporting
Q15 — Growth: What is your primary goal for the next 12 months?
Chips — Survive and stabilize / Increase revenue / Reduce owner dependency / Build a team / Automate and scale / Exit or sell
