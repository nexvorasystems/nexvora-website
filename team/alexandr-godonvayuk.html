About Alexandr

Alexandr Godonvayuk is the Co-Founder of Nexvora Systems and a business development and customer experience consultant based in Tampa Bay, Florida. His work is grounded in the practical experience of building a real business from the ground up — not in theory, and not from the outside looking in.

Before Nexvora, Alexandr co-founded 24 25 CLEANERS alongside Murat Zhandaurov. While Murat focused on the operational and systems side, Alexandr built the business development and customer experience infrastructure that allowed the company to grow to over 20,000 completed jobs. He was responsible for the client-facing side of the business — acquisition, retention, quality standards, and the systems that kept customers coming back.

That experience gave him a deep understanding of how small businesses live or die by the quality of their customer relationships — and how hard it is to maintain consistency when the team grows and the owner can no longer be involved in every interaction.

What He Does at Nexvora

At Nexvora Systems, Alexandr focuses on the business development and customer experience side of consulting. He works with small business owners to identify where customers are being lost, where revenue opportunities are being missed, and how to build systems that create consistent, repeatable customer experiences at scale.

His work spans sales system design, customer journey mapping, retention and referral program development, onboarding improvement, and growth strategy. He also advises on marketing systems and lead generation processes — drawing on direct experience building and running campaigns for a service business.

Like Murat, Alexandr works with clients across all 50 states. Consultations are available online and in person for clients in the Tampa Bay area and Florida.

His Approach

Alexandr's approach to consulting is built on one principle: the best system is the one that actually gets used. He does not build complexity for its own sake. He builds what the business needs to operate without the owner having to personally manage every customer relationship.

  • He focuses on the customer experience gaps that are costing the business repeat business and referrals
  • He designs sales and follow-up systems that work even when the owner is not personally involved
  • He builds retention programs that make existing customers more valuable without requiring more marketing spend
  • He creates onboarding processes that set the right expectations from day one

Background

Alexandr's background in the home services industry gave him exposure to one of the most customer-intensive business environments that exists. Managing customer expectations, handling complaints, building loyalty in a competitive market, and scaling quality across a growing team — these are skills that translate directly to the small businesses Nexvora serves.

Having met hundreds of small business owners across different industries and markets, Alexandr has seen the same patterns repeat consistently: most businesses are not losing because of bad products or bad service. They are losing because they have no system for turning a first customer into a long-term relationship.
