About Murat

Murat Zhandaurov is the Co-Founder of Nexvora Systems and a business operations consultant based in Tampa Bay, Florida. His consulting work draws directly from over a decade of hands-on experience building, running, and systematizing a real small business — not from a textbook or a corporate career.

Before Nexvora, Murat co-founded 24 25 CLEANERS, a home service company that grew to complete over 20,000 jobs and serve thousands of customers across the country. He led the operational side of the business — managing teams, designing processes, handling quality control, and eventually implementing AI and automation systems that fundamentally changed how the company ran.

That experience — the chaos of early growth, the grind of managing people, the trial and error of building systems from scratch — is the foundation of everything Murat brings to Nexvora's clients. He understands what it is like to be the only person in the business who knows how everything works, and what it costs when you try to scale without the infrastructure to support it.

What He Does at Nexvora

At Nexvora Systems, Murat focuses on operational consulting — working directly with small business owners to identify where their business is losing time, money, and growth potential, and then building the systems and processes to fix it. His work spans operations audits, process documentation, AI tool implementation, team accountability systems, and growth strategy.

Murat works with clients across all 50 states. Consultations are available online via video call and in person for clients in the Tampa Bay area and surrounding Florida markets including St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Sarasota, and Orlando.

His Approach

Murat's consulting style is direct and practical. He does not believe in lengthy strategy documents that sit in a drawer. His goal is to find the two or three changes that will have the most immediate impact on a business, implement them, and then build from there.

  • He starts with a free assessment to understand where the biggest gaps are
  • He delivers a prioritized report within 24 hours showing what to fix first
  • He works alongside the owner to implement solutions — not just recommend them
  • He builds systems that the business can operate without constant supervision

Background

Murat built his operational expertise in the home services industry — one of the most demanding environments for small business operations. Managing scheduling, quality, customer expectations, team accountability, and cash flow at scale in a service business requires the same skills that any small business owner needs, just under more pressure and with less room for error.

He has worked directly with thousands of customers across different markets, managed teams in high-volume environments, and navigated the same financial and operational pressures his clients face. That background gives him credibility and context that most consultants simply do not have.
