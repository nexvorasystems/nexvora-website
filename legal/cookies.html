Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026  |  Website: www.nexvorasystems.us

1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Nexvora Systems uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. Cookies help us operate the site, understand how visitors interact with it, and support our marketing efforts. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your browser, remember preferences, and improve performance. In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies including pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, and SDKs. We refer to all of these collectively as "cookies and tracking technologies."

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Operate and secure the website
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand website traffic and usage patterns
  • Measure and improve marketing effectiveness
  • Support advertising and retargeting where applicable
  • Connect website activity to our CRM and lead management tools

4. Categories of Cookies We Use

A. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for core site functions including security, fraud prevention, network management, and basic page functionality. These cannot be disabled through our site tools.

B. Performance and Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website including pages viewed, time on page, traffic sources, and site errors. We may use Google Analytics or similar services.

C. Functional Cookies

Support enhanced functionality and personalization such as remembering preferences and improving user experience based on prior interactions.

D. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

Used to deliver more relevant ads, measure ad performance, track conversions, and support remarketing campaigns. These may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners including Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram).

5. Third-Party Cookies and Tracking

Some cookies may be placed by third parties when you interact with our website. We do not control how third parties use their tracking technologies. Their use is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies. Third parties that may place cookies include analytics providers, advertising networks, CRM providers, and hosting or performance providers.

6. Your Choices and Controls

A. Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to delete existing cookies, block cookies, or receive warnings before cookies are stored. If you disable cookies, some website features may not function properly.

B. Advertising Controls

You may be able to control how advertising partners use your information through their own settings. Depending on your state, you may have the right to opt out of targeted advertising. To submit a request, contact info@nexvorasystems.us.

C. Email Tracking

Marketing emails we send may include open and click tracking. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Because there is no consistent industry standard for responding to "Do Not Track" browser signals, our website may not respond to these signals.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at info@nexvorasystems.us.
