Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026  |  Website: www.nexvorasystems.us

1. Introduction

Nexvora Systems is a U.S.-based business consulting firm committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect data when you interact with our website, applications, strategy sessions, consulting services, and communications.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Website or social media links
  • Industry, revenue, and employee information
  • Business challenges and goals
  • Payment information (where applicable)
  • Form submission content

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address, browser type, and device type
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and referring URLs
  • Interaction behavior on the site

We collect this information through cookies, pixels, analytics tools, and similar technologies.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, Google Analytics, advertising pixels (including Meta/Facebook Pixel), CRM tracking, and marketing automation tools to improve website performance, analyze visitor behavior, measure marketing effectiveness, and deliver relevant advertising. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to review applications, schedule and conduct strategy sessions, deliver consulting services, communicate with users, send marketing and business updates, improve our website and services, comply with legal obligations, and protect our legal rights.

5. Communication and Marketing Consent

By submitting information on this website, you expressly consent to receive communications from Nexvora Systems including phone calls, text messages (SMS), emails, automated reminders, and marketing communications. Message and data rates may apply. Consent to receive communications is not a condition of purchasing services.

To opt out: reply STOP to any SMS, click unsubscribe in any email, or contact us at info@nexvorasystems.us. We comply with the TCPA, CAN-SPAM Act, and applicable state privacy regulations.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with third-party service providers including CRM platforms, email marketing providers, SMS communication providers, payment processors, hosting providers, and analytics platforms. These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your information.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to comply with legal process, to protect our legal rights, or to prevent fraud.

7. Third-Party Tools and Platforms

Nexvora Systems does not own, control, or operate third-party systems we may use or recommend. Each maintains its own privacy policies. We disclaim responsibility for data handling, security vulnerabilities, regulatory compliance, data breaches, or service interruptions within third-party systems. Direct complaints about third-party platforms to those platforms directly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for business purposes, providing consulting services, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and agreement enforcement.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. By using our website, you acknowledge this risk.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have rights under applicable laws including the CCPA/CPRA (California), VCDPA (Virginia), Florida Digital Bill of Rights, and other state privacy statutes. These rights may include access to your data, correction, deletion, portability, and opting out of targeted advertising. We do not sell personal information as defined under applicable state laws. To exercise your rights, contact info@nexvorasystems.us.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and professionals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we discover such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it. Report concerns to info@nexvorasystems.us.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their policies independently.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@nexvorasystems.us.
